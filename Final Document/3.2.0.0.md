### 3.2.1 First Feature

| Data          | Description |
|:--------------| :--------------|
|Time Clock  | Ability to record the amount of time spent on each class/assignment to understand which classes eat up most of your time.|
|Recurring Appointments | Should be able to have option to set events to reoccur weekly and on more than one day of the week.|
|Grades | See what scores you got on assignments and your overall grade. See how much assignments are worth and how they will impact your overal grade.|
|Widget | Widget support for Android. Have a widget that shows upcoming due dates.|
